— Our Story

Why FRALEN

FRALEN was built on a simple idea: everyone deserves a frame that fits their life — not just their face. From a student's first pair to an executive's boardroom frame, from a child's first glasses to a craftsman's titanium piece built to last decades.

We organized FRALEN into seven focused collections so that whatever your budget, lifestyle, or style — there's a frame engineered specifically for you, not a compromise.

Every frame is backed by an 11-month warranty and available both at optical stores across Mumbai and online, because good vision shouldn't be hard to find.
